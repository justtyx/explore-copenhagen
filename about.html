About us

CONCEPT

We are Justina, Agata, Maria and Caroline. Curious, adventurous and always in pursuit of great sights, events and happenings in the bustling city of Copenhagen. We have created this project website as a way for us to share all the best the city has to offer to any other foreign newcomers who needs a reliable, direct source for all the relevant information needed, displayed in a simple format.

PURPOSE

We want to give you the inside scoop on the latest hot spots, hidden gems and all the other wonderful things there is to see in the city, through the eyes of a true local. Arriving in a new country can be an at times overwhelming and daunting task, and it is in our interest to make that journey and transition a bit easier. We hope you'll all find our site useful and can help bring you one step closer to understand the community, cutlure and gain you many newcomers one of a kind and memorable experiences in Copenhagen.
